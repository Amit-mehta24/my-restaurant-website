Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Served with Pride

Ember & Spice was born from a love of fire — the slow burn of a tandoor, the crackle of a woodfire grill, and the warmth of a shared meal.

Every dish on our menu is a story: spices sourced directly from family farms, recipes passed down through generations, and techniques refined over decades. We believe the best food carries the memory of the hands that made it.
